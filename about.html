ABOUT US

WE ARE MAGIC EXPORTS

Our organization strives to enrich the textile market by offering top-of-the-line products with captivating colors and aesthetically pleasing designs. We focus on manufacturing an extensive range of premium-quality textile products such as baskets, bags, decorative poufs, and an assortment of accessories that appeal to our customers and help us build a relationship with them.

With customer satisfaction at the helm of our list of goals, we have ensured that we reach them by implementing the latest technology and utilizing state-of-the-art infrastructure to streamline our operation assembly.

OUR MISSION

Our mission statement prioritizes on our organization having strict quality control norms, scheduled on-time dispatches and deliveries and transparent business policies so that we can create high value solutions to our customers.
We at The Magic Exports always stay updated with technological advancements in the textile industry so we can focus on innovating exceptional designs and craft the best products. Our operations run smoothly and efficiently so that our excellent solutions reach our customers on-time and consolidate our reliability with them.

SUSTAINABILITY

We aspire to be a sustainable company that contributes to the replenishment of nature hence, we have tried to make our manufacturing processes as environment-friendly as we can. Being a part of the Better Cotton Initiative, often called BCI, we promote sustainable agricultural practices to ensure exceptional standards in cotton farming. Recycling is another step that we have taken to become more sustainable; we make use of cotton made from recycled plastic in the manufacture of some of our products. To reduce the use of plastic in packaging, we utilize bags, hang tags and cardboard boxes or cartons to pack and ship our products.
